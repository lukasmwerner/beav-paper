Most students collect links faster than they revisit them.

A simple resources section fixes that by turning scattered links into curated pages.

## Good resource page ideas

- interview prep checklist
- favorite OSU CS links
- study tools you recommend
- internship application timeline
- textbook alternatives or references

## Keep the page useful

For each resource, explain:

- who it helps
- when to use it
- why it is worth bookmarking

That extra sentence matters more than the link itself.
