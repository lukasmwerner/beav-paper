This project started as a way to make campus involvement easier for new students.

## Goals

- help students browse clubs quickly
- reduce the friction of outdated link lists
- make student organizations easier to discover

## What I built

I designed a simple directory with filters for category, meeting time, and level of commitment.

## What I learned

The hardest part was not the UI. It was keeping the data clean and deciding what information mattered most to students.
